# 2020 Shareholder Letter

**Date:** February 2021
**Author:** Warren Buffett
**Company:** Berkshire Hathaway
**Source:** [2020 Letter (PDF)](https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/letters/2020ltr.pdf)

## Overview

Written during the COVID-19 pandemic, this letter contains critical lessons about crisis management and Berkshire's unprecedented moves (selling airline stocks, holding record cash).

## Key Points

### The COVID Crisis

Buffett explained the unique nature of the 2020 crisis and Berkshire's response.

### Major Decisions

- Sold airline stocks (Southwest, etc.) — First time selling in a crisis
- Held record cash position ($140 billion+)
- No major acquisitions despite low prices

### Famous Quotes

> "The most important thing to do in a crisis is to maintain your liquidity and be ready to act when others are panicking."
