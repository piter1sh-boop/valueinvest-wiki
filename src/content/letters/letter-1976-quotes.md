# Key Quotes from 1976 Shareholder Letter

1. > "Casualty insurers enjoyed some rebound from the disaster levels of 1975 as rate increases finally outstripped relentless cost increases."

2. > "Our overall improvement was even more dramatic, from 115.4 to 98.7."

3. > "It presently appears that 1977 also will be a year of net realized capital gains. We now have a substantial unrealized gain in our stock portfolio as compared to a substantial unrealized loss several years ago."

4. > "We are unusually concentrated in auto lines where stock companies had an improvement from 113.5 to 107.4."
