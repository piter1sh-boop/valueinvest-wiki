# Key Quotes from 2001 Shareholder Letter

1. > "Berkshire's loss in net worth during 2001 was $3.77 billion."

2. > "The world was different after 9/11 — changed in ways that will be with us a long time, in ways we could not have imagined before that Tuesday morning."

3. > "In an era of stable wages and prices, compound interest works its magic... But inflation is the arch-enemy of the investor."

4. > "The insurance business has taught me that 'float' can be dangerous if managed by people who have either evil intentions or poor judgment."
