# Key Quotes from 2002 Shareholder Letter

1. > "In all respects 2002 was a banner year."

2. > "We've yet to see a pro-forma presentation disclosing that audited earnings were somewhat high. So let's make a little history: Last year, on a pro-forma basis, Berkshire had *lower* earnings than those we actually reported."

3. > "When valuations are similar, we strongly prefer owning businesses to owning stocks. During most of our years of operation, however, stocks were much the cheaper choice."

4. > "'Except for' losses will forever be part of the insurance business, and they will forever be paid with shareholders' money."
