# Key Quotes from 1987 Shareholder Letter

1. > "In many cases, a corporation's book value and business value are almost totally unrelated."

2. > "Our premium of business value to book value has widened for two simple reasons: We own some remarkable businesses and they are run by even more remarkable managers."

3. > "Jack Benny expressed upon receiving an acting award: 'I don't deserve this, but then, I have arthritis and I don't deserve that either.'"
