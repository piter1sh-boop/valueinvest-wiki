# Key Quotes from 1999 Shareholder Letter

1. > "The numbers on the facing page show just how poor our 1999 record was. We had the worst absolute performance of my tenure and, compared to the S&P, the worst relative performance as well."

2. > "We bought both for cash, issuing no Berkshire shares. Deals of that kind aren't always possible, but that is the method of acquisition that Charlie and I vastly prefer."

3. > "For Berkshire, truly large superiorities over that index are a thing of the past."

4. > "I think you're spending too much time on that one subject."
