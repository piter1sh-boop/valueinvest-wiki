# Key Quotes from 2013 Shareholder Letter

1. > "Book value is a useful tracking device, but 'significantly understated' proxy for intrinsic value."

2. > "We have underperformed in ten of our 49 years, with all but one of our shortfalls occurring when the S&P gain exceeded 15%."

3. > "What counts is per-share intrinsic value. But that's a subjective figure, and book value is useful as a rough tracking indicator."
