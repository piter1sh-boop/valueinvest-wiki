# 1974 Shareholder Letter - Summary

1974 delivered **10.3% return on beginning shareholders' equity** — the lowest ROE since 1970. The poor results were driven by catastrophic insurance underwriting deterioration, while textile and banking operations performed well. The letter provides a clear-eyed analysis of why insurance costs were rising 1% monthly while premiums remained flat.
