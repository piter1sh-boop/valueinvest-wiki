# Key Quotes from 1988 Shareholder Letter

1. > "Four years ago I told you that we needed profits of $3.9 billion to achieve a 15% annual return over the decade then ahead. Today, for the next decade, a 15% return demands profits of $10.3 billion."

2. > "That seems like a very big number to me and to Charlie Munger."

3. > "Without financial leverage, this group earned about **67% on average equity capital**."

4. > "The major problem we face, however, is a growing capital base... And also, just as with age, it's better to have this problem continue to grow rather than to have it 'solved.'"
