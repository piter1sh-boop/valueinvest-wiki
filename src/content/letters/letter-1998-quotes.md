# Key Quotes from 1998 Shareholder Letter

1. > "Remember Wagner, whose music has been described as better than it sounds? Well, Berkshire's progress in 1998 -- though more than satisfactory -- was not as good as it looks."

2. > "GEICO, once again, simply shot the lights out."

3. > "Popcorn stands just won't do. Today's markets are not friendly to our search for 'elephants,' but you can be sure that we will stay focused on the hunt."

4. > "I'll keep at least 99% of my net worth in Berkshire for as long as I am around."

5. > "There are really only three kinds of people in the world: those who can count and those who can't."
