# Charlie Munger on Acquired Podcast

**Date:** October 2023
**Event:** Acquired Podcast Interview
**Source:** [Acquired FM](https://www.acquired.fm/episodes/charlie-munger)

## Overview

This was Charlie Munger's **only dedicated long-form podcast interview** in his 99 years. Recorded at his Los Angeles home over dinner, Munger reflected on his career, his nearly 50-year partnership with [[warren-buffett|Warren Buffett]] at [[berkshire-hathaway|Berkshire Hathaway]], and shared timeless investment wisdom.

## Key Discussion Points

### On Building the Berkshire Partnership

Munger described how Berkshire evolved:

> "We never tried to steal from each other or compete in ways that would undermine the other. We just found that together we could make more than separately, and it grew from there."

The partnership worked because both had **skin in the game** and aligned incentives. Munger credits much of their success to having a **shared philosophy** and **trust**.

### Costco: The Extraordinary Business

Munger reiterated his passion for [[costco]]:

> "Costco is the most ethical retailer on Earth. They pass virtually all the savings to customers. And the customers keep coming back. That's a wonderful business."

He emphasized that Costco's [[moat]] comes from:
- **Ethical culture** — Sincerely helping customers
- **Operational efficiency** — Barely sustainable margins that competitors can't match
- **Customer loyalty** — People drive 100 miles to shop there

### On Investment Opportunities Today

When asked where opportunities remain:

> "There are always opportunities. But the easy money has been made. You have to work harder, think deeper, and be willing to be contrary."

Munger cautioned about current market conditions:

> "We've created a system where the smartest people go into finance and try to outsmart each other. That's not good for civilization."

### Investment vs. Gambling

Munger drew a clear line:

| Gambling | Investing |
|----------|----------|
| Transfers wealth | Creates wealth |
| Zero-sum game | Positive-sum game |
| Requires luck | Requires skill and patience |

> "The casino always wins because it has the house edge. The question for investors is: Do you have an edge? And can you protect it?"

### On His Formula for Success

> "The first thing is: Don't do things that will destroy you. The second thing is: Recognize when you're wrong and change course quickly."

## Famous Quotes

> "The best partnerships are built on trust and aligned incentives, not clever contracts."

> "There are always opportunities. But you have to be willing to think independently and be patient."
