# Buffett Partnership Letter 1964 Annual

**Warren E. Buffett** | January 18, 1965

## 1964 Performance

| Year | Dow | Partnership | Limited Partners |
|------|-----|------------|-----------------|
| 1957 | -8.4% | 10.4% | 9.3% |
| 1958 | 38.5% | 40.9% | 32.2% |
| 1959 | 20.0% | 25.9% | 20.9% |
| 1960 | -6.2% | 22.8% | 18.6% |
| 1961 | 22.4% | 45.9% | 35.9% |
| 1962 | -7.6% | 13.9% | 11.9% |
| 1963 | 20.7% | 38.7% | 30.5% |
| 1964 | 18.7% | 27.8% | 22.3% |

## Key Results

- Overall gain: **$4,846,312**
- Partnership gained **27.8%**
- Dow gained **18.7%**
- 9.1 point advantage over Dow (poorest margin since 1959)

## 8-Year Cumulative Performance

| Metric | Dow | Partnership | Limited Partners |
|--------|-----|------------|-----------------|
| Cumulative | 131.3% | 608.7% | 402.9% |
| Annual Compounded | 11.1% | **27.7%** | **22.3%** |

## On Performance Expectations

> "In my judgment, our 17.7 margin over the Dow shown above is unattainable over any long period of time. A ten percentage point advantage would be a very satisfactory accomplishment."
