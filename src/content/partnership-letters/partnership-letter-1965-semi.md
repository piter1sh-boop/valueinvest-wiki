# Buffett Partnership Letter 1965 Semi

**Warren E. Buffett** | July 9, 1965

## First Half 1965 Performance

| Metric | Dow | Partnership | Limited Partners |
|--------|-----|------------|-----------------|
| First Half 1965 | +0.8% | +10.4% | +9.3% |

- **9.6 percentage point advantage** over Dow

## Market Context

The Dow reached a high of 939.62 on May 14th before declining to 868.03 by June 30th.

> "We actually achieved a wide margin during the upswing and then fell at a rate fully equal to the Dow during the market decline."

## Control Situation Acquired

During the first half, Buffett acquired controlling interest in one of the situations previously described in the "General Private Owner" category.

> "When a controlling interest is held, we own a business rather than a stock, and a business valuation is appropriate."

The business operation and acquisition cost were both satisfactory, with excellent management in place.
