# Buffett Partnership Letter October 1969

**Warren E. Buffett** | October 9, 1969

## Introducing Bill Ruane

Buffett recommends Bill Ruane (Ruane, Cunniff & Stires) to partners needing an alternative money manager.

**Bill's credentials:**
- Met in Ben Graham's class at Columbia University, 1951
- Composite record: 1956-1961 and 1964-1968 averaged over 40% per annum
- 1962 was down about 50%, 1963 was breakeven
- Manages $20-30 million currently

> "I consider Bill to be an exceptionally high probability decision on character and a high probability one on investment performance."

## Bond vs Stock Analysis

For a taxpayer in 40% Federal bracket:

| Investment | Expected After-Tax Return |
|------------|--------------------------|
| Passive tax-free bonds | 6.5% |
| Professionally managed stocks (average) | 6.5% |
| Top 1-2% managed stocks | 9.5% |

> "For the first time in my investment lifetime, I now believe there is little choice for the average investor between professionally managed money in stocks and passive investment in bonds."

## Liquidation Timeline

- **November**: Formal 30-day notice of retirement
- **December**: Information package on controlled companies (DRC and B-H)
- **January 5**: Cash distribution (at least 56% of Jan 1, 1969 capital) + proportional DRC/B-H shares
- **March 1970**: Bill and John available for bond purchases
- **After March 1970**: Bill and Warren out of advisory business
