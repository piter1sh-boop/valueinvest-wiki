# 1986 Annual Meeting (Kingswell Reconstruction)

**Date:** 1986
**Location:** Joslyn Art Museum, Omaha (first year at this venue)
**Source:** [Kingswell](https://www.kingswell.io/p/rediscovering-berkshire-hathaways)

## Overview

This is a Kingswell reconstruction of Berkshire Hathaway's 1986 annual meeting, one of the earliest recorded meetings. The meeting moved to the Joslyn Art Museum auditorium this year due to growing attendance.

## Key Highlights

### Venue Change
After years at the Red Lion Inn, 1986 was the first year the meeting moved to the larger Joslyn Art Museum auditorium due to increasing attendance.

### Topics Discussed
As with other early meetings, topics included:
- Investment philosophy
- Business performance of Berkshire's operating companies
- Insurance operations
- Q&A with shareholders
