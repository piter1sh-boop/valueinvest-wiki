# Famous Quotes from 2009 Annual Meeting

1. > "We've been through a financial Pearl Harbor. The system nearly collapsed."

2. > "Treasury and Fed acted boldly. Without them, it would have been worse."

3. > "We didn't panic. We kept investing."

4. > "We had cash when others needed it. That's when opportunities appear."

5. > "Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful."

6. > "We invested in Goldman because they had the best franchise in the world."

7. > "For the first time in my life, great businesses were available at ridiculous prices."

8. > "Derivatives are weapons of mass financial destruction."

9. > "They're like weapons that can detonate anywhere in the system."

10. > "Too much leverage everywhere. People forgot about risk."
