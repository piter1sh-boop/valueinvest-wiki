# 1987 Annual Meeting (Kingswell Reconstruction)

**Date:** 1987
**Location:** Joslyn Art Museum, Omaha
**Source:** [Kingswell](https://www.kingswell.io/p/rediscovering-berkshire-hathaways)

## Overview

This is a Kingswell reconstruction of Berkshire Hathaway's 1987 annual meeting. This was the last year before CNBC began archiving the meetings in 1994.

## Key Highlights

### Investment Philosophy
Even at this stage, Buffett and Munger were teaching the timeless principles that would guide investors for decades.

### Pre-Black Monday
This meeting took place before the October 1987 stock market crash (Black Monday), providing insight into the mindset before the crash.
