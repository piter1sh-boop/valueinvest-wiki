# Chuck Huggins

**Chuck Huggins** was the manager of See's Candy Shops who ran the legendary candy company during its formative years under Blue Chip Stamps and Berkshire Hathaway ownership. Under his leadership, See's Candy grew from $4.2 million to $12.6 million in pre-tax earnings.

## Career

### Managing See's

Chuck Huggins ran See's Candy during its key growth years:
- Maintained the brand's quality and reputation
- Expanded the store network
- Demonstrated the pricing power that made See's valuable

### The See's Candy Principle

Huggins' management of See's illustrated Buffett's key test:
- Could raise prices without losing customers
- Demonstrated durable competitive advantages
- Generated exceptional returns on capital
