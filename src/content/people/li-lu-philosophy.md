# Li Lu - Investment Philosophy

### Value Investing as a Natural Path

Li Lu describes value investing as a "正道" (right path) that he never strayed from:

> "I never felt tempted by other methods. Some ways to make quick money or through insider information to make money from others have never had any attraction for me."

### The Role of Luck

Li Lu emphasizes the role of luck in success:

> "At least in my life, and in the lives of all successful people I know, luck has played a very significant role."
