# Famous Quotes from Seth Klarman

1. > "The margin of safety is the difference between the price and the intrinsic value. It exists when the price is significantly below the value."

2. > "In investing, it is only necessary to be right about the direction of the general market, and then to buy and sell intelligently."

3. > "The most risky thing you can do is be careless about the price you pay."

4. > "Value investing is the discipline of buying shares at a discount from underlying net value, with the expectation that a catalyst will unlock that value."
