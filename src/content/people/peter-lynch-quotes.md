# Famous Quotes from Peter Lynch

1. > "Never invest in a company that you don't fully understand."

2. > "The stock market is filled with individuals who know the price of everything, but the value of nothing."

3. > "If you can't find companies you understand, put your money in index funds."

4. > "In this business, if you're good, you're right six times out of ten. You're never right nine times out of ten."
