# Terry Smith - Investment Philosophy

### Three Principles

1. **Only invest in good companies** — Quality over everything
2. **Try not to overpay** — Valuation matters, even for great businesses
3. **Do nothing** — Extremely low turnover (3.2% in 2024, costing just £447,000)
