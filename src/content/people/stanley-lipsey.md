# Stanley Lipsey

**Stanley Lipsey** (commonly called Stan Lipsey) was the publisher of the Buffalo Evening News, which Berkshire acquired in 1977. Lipsey ran the newspaper with the same discipline and focus on long-term value that characterized all of Buffett's operations.

## Career

### Buffalo Evening News

The Buffalo Evening News was Berkshire's entry into newspapers:
- Acquired in 1977
- One of the few profitable newspapers in the region
- Lipsey maintained profitability through the newspaper industry's challenges

### Management Philosophy

Lipsey demonstrated:
- Cost control discipline
- Focus on core readership
- Long-term thinking about newspaper economics
