# Louis Vincenti

**Louis Vincenti** was the president of Wesco Financial Corporation, running the conservative savings and loan association with the same careful approach that characterized all of Buffett's insurance operations.

## Overview

| Metric | Value |

## Career

### Leading Wesco

Vincenti ran Wesco Financial with conservative principles:
- Maintained strong capital adequacy
- Focused on quality lending
- Operated independently but aligned with Buffett

### Wesco's Role

Wesco was part of the Blue Chip Stamps affiliate network:
- 80% owned by Blue Chip
- Operated under Charlie Munger's oversight
- Demonstrated the "cigar butt" value investing approach
