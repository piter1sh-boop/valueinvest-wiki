# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2020

**Warren Buffett (alone)** | May 2020

## Overview

- **Format**: Virtual meeting due to COVID-19
- **Charlie Munger**: Not present (96 years old, using Zoom)
- **Greg Abel**: Present as vice chairman

## Key Topics

### COVID-19 Reflections

Buffett discussed the unprecedented nature of the pandemic:
> "This is quite an experiment."

He expressed confidence in Dr. Anthony Fauci and acknowledged the uncertainty around health and economic outcomes.

### America's Resilience

Buffett's central message was America's ability to overcome challenges:

> "Nothing can basically stop America."

He traced American history showing how the country overcame:
- Civil War (6% of working-age males lost)
- Great Depression
- World War II

### Wealth Creation

Buffett calculated that America's wealth has grown approximately 5,000-for-1 since 1789.

### Investment Philosophy

He emphasized buying businesses, not stocks:

> "We look at every stock as a business."
