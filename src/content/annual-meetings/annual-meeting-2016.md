# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2016

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2016

## Overview

- **First webcast** with Mandarin translation
- **Theme**: Investment lessons

## Key Topics

### First Webcast

Buffett welcomed international viewers:
> "We're having this meeting simultaneously translated into Mandarin. And that poses certain problems for me and Charlie, because I'm not sure how sensible all our comments will come out once translated into Mandarin."

### Most Important Investment Lesson

Buffett announced he'd share what he considers "the most important investment lesson in the world" at noon.

### Q1 Results

- Insurance underwriting down due to Texas hailstorms
- Railroad earnings down significantly with industry car loadings
- New acquisitions: Precision Castparts and Duracell added

### Tax Rate Impact

Buffett discussed how the lower tax rate would affect business earnings.
