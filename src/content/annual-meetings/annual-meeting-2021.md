# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2021

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2021

## Overview

- **Theme**: Second virtual annual meeting from Los Angeles
- **Notable**: Held with Charlie Munger in LA; vice chairmen introduced

## Key Topics

### Charlie Munger Introduction

Buffett shared personal history:
> "I met Charlie 62 years ago. He was practicing law in Los Angeles. He was building a house at that time a few miles from here. And 62 years later, he's still living in the same house."

On Munger's role:
> "He's probably the vice chairman in charge of culture, among other things."

### Greg Abel Introduction

> "Greg was born and raised in Edmonton, Alberta. He's Canadian. Plays hockey. His eight-year-old plays hockey."

> "He is in charge of a business which has well over $150 billion in sales and employs more than 250,000 people. And does a much better job at doing that than I was doing previously."

### Ajit Jain Introduction

> "I met Ajit on a Saturday in 1986. And I'd been in the insurance business for quite a while. And I was kind of stumbling around in various ways. And Ajit came to the office. And Saturday, I was opening the mail."

Buffett recalled asking Ajit "How much do you know about insurance?" Ajit replied "Nothing." Buffett: "Well, nobody's perfect."

The result:
> "That improbable little company in Omaha became the largest property-casualty company in the world, in terms of net worth."

### Omaha Connection

All four Berkshire leaders lived near Buffett in Omaha at some point — Charlie in 1934 (100 yards away), Greg several blocks away, Ajit for a couple years.

### Reading the 10-Q

Buffett recommended reading the full 10-Q:
> "If you're really a student of the place... if you really enjoy going into the details and you want to understand the nuts and bolts of Berkshire Hathaway's various operations, you should read that 10-Q."

On quarterly losses:
> "First quarter, we actually showed a loss of almost $50 billion... And neither figure is very meaningful in itself."
