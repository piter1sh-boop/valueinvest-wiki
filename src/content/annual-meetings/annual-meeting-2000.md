# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2000

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2000

## Overview

- **Theme**: Venue transition as Ak-Sar-Ben was winding down
- **Notable**: Berkshire now had ~60,000 employees with only 12.8 at headquarters

## Key Topics

### Berkshire's Scale

> "As you saw in the movie, I think, at the time, we may have had 45,000 or so people working with Berkshire, with 12.8 at headquarters. We're probably up to about 60,000 now, and we still have 12.8, and they take care of putting on this whole meeting."

### Venue Change

Ak-Sar-Ben Coliseum (with keno and racetracks) was "winding down":
> "We had about the same number of ticket requests as in the past, but we had a different mix this year. We — as most of you know — we had change the venue, and the time, because Ak-Sar-Ben is winding down."

### Thank You

Kelly Muchemore processed 25,000 ticket requests. Marc Hamburg coordinated exhibitors.

### Meeting Format

- 9:30 to 12:00 business and Q&A
- Break for lunch
- 12:45 to 3:30 Q&A continuation
- 10 microphone stations around the room
