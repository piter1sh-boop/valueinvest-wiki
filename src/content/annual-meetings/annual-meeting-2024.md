# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2024

**Warren Buffett** | May 2024

## Overview

- **Notable**: First meeting honoring Charlie Munger (who passed away in 2023)
- **Bookstore**: Only "Poor Charlie's Almanack" (4th edition) was sold - 2,400 copies
- **Format**: Tribute to Munger throughout

## Key Topics

### Honoring Charlie Munger

Buffett showed a tribute film for Munger and introduced directors.

### Cash Position

Berkshire's cash position:
- $182 billion at quarter end
- Likely ~$200 billion by end of Q2
- Seeking big opportunities with "very little risk"

### Apple Investment

Buffett explained why Berkshire sold some Apple shares:
- Paid 21% federal tax on gains
- Said higher taxes are "quite likely" in the future

> "We don't mind paying taxes at Berkshire."

### Investment Philosophy

Buffett emphasized treating stocks as businesses:

> "We look at common stocks or marketable equities as businesses."

He reiterated his faith in Apple's business quality.

### Berkshire's Economic Goal

1. Increase operating earnings
2. Decrease shares outstanding

### Succession

Greg Abel will take over and Buffett expects Apple, American Express, and Coca-Cola to remain holdings.
