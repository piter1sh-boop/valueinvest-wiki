# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2006

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2006

## Overview

- **Notable**: Anna Nicole Smith joke; Chuck Huggins tribute; Secret Millionaires Club preview

## Key Topics

### Anna Nicole Smith Rule

Buffett joked about why Munger "always gets the girl" in videos:
> "It's something called the 'Anna Nicole Smith Rule.' That's when choosing between two old rich guys, pick the older one."

### See's Candy and Chuck Huggins

Buffett credited See's acquisition with changing his thinking about intangibles:
> "Prior to the purchase of See's Candies in 1972, I intended to look primarily at financial measures in buying businesses... But we found out that the intangible assets, if properly nourished... you can make a whole lot more money with than buying a lot of tangible assets cheap."

Chuck Huggins was introduced as the ideal manager who has run See's since 1972.

### Directors with Skin in the Game

> "I know of no directors of any large, publicly-owned companies that have, universally, as significant a percentage of their net worth in the company, purchased in the open market, as that group."

Munger confirmed: "None."

### Secret Millionaires Club

Andy Heyward was working on a new animated program to teach children about money:
> "It's going to be a program that's designed to teach young people some of the very basic lessons of — about money. How to avoid getting trouble with it, how to use it effectively, and what your attitude should be toward it."

### Directors

Howard Buffett, Malcolm Chace, Bill Gates, David Gottesman, Charlotte Guyman, Don Keough, Tom Murphy, Ron Olson, Walter Scott Jr.
