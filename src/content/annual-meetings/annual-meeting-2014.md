# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2014

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2014

## Overview

- **Notable guests**: Paul Anka attended; Carrie Sova organized the event
- **Key theme**: Shareholder democracy and dividend policy

## Key Topics

### Dividend Vote

Buffett celebrated shareholders rejecting a dividend proposal:
- Class A vote: 90-to-1 against dividend
- Class B vote: 45-to-1 against dividend

> "I'm not sure there's any company in the world that would get quite that vote."

Buffett joked that the vote showed shareholders would choose him over dividend payments.

### Apple Shares

Berkshire had sold some Apple shares at a gain, paying 21% federal tax rate.

### Coca-Cola Compensation

Buffett defended abstaining (rather than voting against) on Coca-Cola's excessive executive compensation plan:
- He informed Coca-Cola CEO Muhtar Kent beforehand
- Made public statement after the vote
- Believed public criticism was more effective than proxy war

### Look at Stocks as Businesses

Buffett reiterated his core investment philosophy:

> "We look at every stock as a business."

He emphasized the importance of ignoring short-term market movements.
