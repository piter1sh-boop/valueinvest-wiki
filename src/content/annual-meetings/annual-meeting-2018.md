# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2018

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2018

## Overview

- **Theme**: Long-term investment perspective and historical context
- **Famous**: Buffett brought a 1942 New York Times to illustrate his first investment

## Key Topics

### The Cities Service Investment (Age 11)

Buffett shared his formative investment experience:
- March 1942: US was losing WWII
- News headlines were terrible (Philippines falling, etc.)
- Cities Service preferred stock fell to $40
- He asked his dad to buy 3 shares with all his savings

> "That was all I had. I mean, that was my capital accumulated over the previous five years or thereabouts."

### Learning from 1942

Buffett contrasted pessimistic headlines with long-term opportunity:
- Despite bad war news, he invested
- The key is focusing on businesses, not headlines

### New Accounting Rules

New GAAP rules mark securities to market daily:
> "We can have a gain or loss of a couple billion dollars in our equity securities portfolio, and that day... will be recorded as making a couple billion dollars that day or losing a couple billion."

### Tax Reform Impact

The reduction from 35% to 21% tax rate significantly helped Q1 earnings.
