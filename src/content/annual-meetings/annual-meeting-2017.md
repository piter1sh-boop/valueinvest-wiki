# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2017

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2017

## Overview

- **Theme**: Omaha roots and local connections
- **Notable**: Buffett family graduated from Central High School

## Key Topics

### Omaha Connections

Buffett emphasized local ties:
> "Both of us were born within two miles of this building... Charlie graduated from Central High, which is about one mile from here."

His dad, first wife, three children, and two grandchildren all graduated from the same school.

### GEICO Growth

GEICO had a remarkable quarter:
- 700,000 new policyholders in first four months (vs 300,000 last year)
- Competitors intentionally cut back on new business due to first-year losses

### Tax Planning

Buffett explained preference for tax losses:
> "We would rather take losses than gains, because of the tax effect if two securities were equally valued."

### Investment vs Speculation

Buffett discussed focusing on business value rather than accounting rules.
