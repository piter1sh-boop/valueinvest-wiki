# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2002

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2002

## Overview

- **Notable**: Susie Buffett received fewest "withhold" votes; Charlie and Warren had ~16,000 votes against

## Key Topics

### Susie Leads the Ticket

Buffett disclosed voting results with humor:
> "In the spirt of disclosure which now permeates the corporate world, I have a tally here from yesterday as to the number of votes each director has received."

> "Susie did the best. She only had 1,000 votes against her, but Charlie and I had 16,000-some votes against us."

> "I really suspect that Susie voted against us so that she could lead the ticket."

### Share Counts

- 1,323,707 Class A shares outstanding
- 6,290,415 Class B shares outstanding (1/200th vote each)

### Directors Elected

Warren E. Buffett, Charles T. Munger, Susan T. Buffett, Howard G. Buffett, Malcolm G. Chace, Ronald L. Olson, and Walter Scott Jr.

### Shareholder Proposal

A proposal from Human Life International on charitable contributions, abortion, and overpopulation was on the agenda.
