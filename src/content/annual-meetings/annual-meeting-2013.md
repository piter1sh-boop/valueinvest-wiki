# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2013

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2013

## Overview

- **Theme**: GEICO excellence and railroad gains
- **Notable**: Berkshire became the fifth most valuable company in the world

## Key Topics

### GEICO Performance

GEICO showed exceptional results:
> "A policy has a mathematical value to us of at least $1,500, so if we had a million policies in a year — and I'm hopeful we might do that this year — that's a billion-and-a-half of value that gets built into our intrinsic value."

- Closure rate improved "very significantly"
- Persistency rate improved — "people that renew the policies with us, and that's pure gold"
- Month-by-month policy gains significantly ahead of 2012

Buffett praised the gecko:
> "People love our little gecko, but they buy the policies because we save them money."

### Railroad Success

BNSF outperforming industry:
> "We've been helped by the fact that, fortunately, a lot of oil has been found very, very close to our railroad tracks."

- BNSF car loadings up 3.8% vs. 0.4% for other Class I railroads
- Moving significant oil volumes from new discoveries near tracks

### Swiss Re Settlement

Accounting quirk from dispute resolution:
> "We showed a gain of 255 million pretax from settling this disagreement, but, interestingly, Swiss Re showed a gain of 100 million also from settling the disagreement."

> "We are working on an arrangement with Swiss Re whether we'll get in an argument every quarter, and both report higher earnings when we settle it."

### Fifth Most Valuable Company

> "The result of all this — we're now the fifth most valuable company in the world."

### Board of Directors

New director added: Meryl Witmer.

Howard Buffett, Steve Burke, Susan Decker, Bill Gates, Sandy Gottesman, Charlotte Guyman, Don Keough, Tom Murphy, Ron Olson, Walter Scott, Jr.
