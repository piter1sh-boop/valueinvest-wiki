# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2007

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2007

## Overview

- **Venue**: Qwest Center (new larger venue)
- **Notable**: Jimmy Buffett surprise performance with custom "Berkshire Hathaway-ville" song

## Key Topics

### Jimmy Buffett's Surprise Appearance

Musician Jimmy Buffett (distant cousin) opened with humor:
> "Who were you expecting? My junior partner?"

On succession:
> "Those guys are getting up in age, you know, Charlie and Warren. Who are they going to leave it to?"

> "We did a genetic test, Warren and I did. Somewhere back about 6,000 years ago, in some ancient village in Scandinavia, they were trading Buffett genes, and I got the talent. He got the business."

Jimmy declared himself the new chairman:
> "So, since blood is thicker than water, I am your new chairman."

### Berkshire Hathaway-ville Song

Sung to the tune of "Margaritaville":
> "Wastin' away in Berkshire Hathaway-a-ville, searchin' for my lost box of See's."

The song referenced World Books, sofas, jet planes, diamonds, and euros.

### Succession Concerns

> "This would be a good day to rob a bank in Omaha. Everybody's here."
