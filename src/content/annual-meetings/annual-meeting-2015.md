# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2015

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2015

## Overview

- **Attendance**: Full house with overflow rooms
- **Notable**: Tribute to Don Keough (passed away)
- **Book**: Carrie Sova produced commemorative 50-year retrospective book

## Key Topics

### BNSF Railroad Turnaround

Buffett praised Matt Rose and Carl Ice for BNSF improvements:
> "We got behind last year, early in the year, and there's been lots of money, and more important, lots of effort, spent to get the railroad operating like it should be."

The railroad showed dramatic improvement in earnings, train velocity, and on-time performance.

### Tribute to Don Keough

Buffett honored longtime director Don Keough:
- Started as a coffee salesman for Butter-Nut Coffee
- Later broadcast Nebraska football games
- Had 15-minute radio show, followed by Johnny Carson

> "Mickie practically raised our kids, so if they have any faults, talk to Mickie about it."

### Directors Introduced

Alphabetically: Howard Buffett, Steve Burke, Sue Decker, Bill Gates, Sandy Gottesman, Charlotte Guyman, Tom Murphy, Ron Olson, Walter Scott, Meryl Witmer
