# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 1996

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 1996

## Overview

- **Attendance**: ~5,400 across main room and three overflow rooms
- **Notable**: At 62% of ticket requesters attending, venue nearly full

## Key Topics

### Venue Capacity Warning

> "We can handle around 5,400. And historically, 62 percent or just about exactly 62 percent, every year, of the people who request tickets have come to the meeting."

> "If that percentage holds true today, we have just filled the rooms. And we will have a problem in the future, which we haven't figured out the answer to yet."

### Staff-Run Meeting

Buffett emphasized no professional PR team:
> "This entire meeting is handled by a regular staff. We have no public relations department, or investor relations, or multimedia department, or anything of the sort. So, everybody just pitches in."

Marc Hamburg (CFO) in charge of pre-game ceremonies.

### International Attendance

Tickets requested from: 99 from Canada, plus Australia, Channel Islands, England, Greece, Hong Kong, Israel, Portugal, Puerto Rico, Singapore, Sweden, Switzerland.

### Meeting Format

- Business meeting first (discussing Class B issuance)
- Q&A until noon
- Lunch break with sandwiches available
- Resumption until ~3 PM
- Attendees encouraged to visit Berkshire exhibits

Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger, 1996 Annual Meeting.
