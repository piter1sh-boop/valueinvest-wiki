# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 1995

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 1995

## Overview

- **Attendance**: ~3,300 in main room
- **Notable**: First mention of GEICO acquisition (completed in 1996)

## Key Topics

### Preferred Stock Item

Buffett discussed a preferred stock issue that caused some shareholder confusion, promising to explain before the vote.

### Directors Introduced

- Susan T. Buffett
- Howard Buffett
- Malcolm Chace, III
- Walter Scott Jr.
- Charles T. Munger
- Warren E. Buffett

Auditors: Deloitte & Touche (Ron Burgess, Craig Christiansen)

### International Attendance

> "We have people from Australia, Israel, Sweden, France, the U.K., 40-some from Canada. So, a lot of people have come a long way."

Only Vermont was absent from the 50 states.

### Q&A Commitment

> "You people are part owners of the company. And any question that relates to your ownership of Berkshire, we want to be able to give you a chance to ask."

Buffett indicated they would stay until 3 PM if needed, but would cut off if crowd dropped below a couple hundred.

### Logistics

Buses available to hotels, airports, and Berkshire commercial establishments.

## Source

Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger, 1995 Annual Meeting.
