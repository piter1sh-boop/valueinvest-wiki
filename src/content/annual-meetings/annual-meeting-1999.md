# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 1999

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 1999

## Overview

- **Attendance**: Packed house, multiple overflow rooms
- **Format**: Q&A session running for 6 hours or until candy runs out

## Key Topics

### General Re Merger

Buffett explained that General Re CEO Ron Ferguson declined to join Berkshire's board:
- The offer remains open "for his lifetime and mine"
- Ferguson preferred not to restrict his activities

### Disadvantages of Corporate Directorships

Buffett discussed why he limits board memberships:
- Being on boards can restrict trading activities
- Six-month look-back rules can cause problems
- Compensation disclosure creates issues

> "It has cost Berkshire significant money by the fact that Charlie and I have been on various boards, because your hands are tied."

### Shareholder Engagement

Buffett emphasized that Berkshire shareholders behave like owners:
> "It says something... about the way you regard yourself as owners."
