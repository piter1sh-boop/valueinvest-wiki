# Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting 2019

**Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger** | 2019

## Overview

- **Theme**: Succession humor and long-term commitments
- **Notable**: Charlie Munger ran "insurgency campaign" with "Vote for Charlie" buttons

## Key Topics

### Succession Joke

Munger distributed buttons saying "Maturity, experience, why accept second best? Vote for Charlie."

Buffett responded:
> "I, however, have appointed the monitors who collect the votes, so I feel very secure."

### 20-Year Lease

Berkshire signed a 20-year lease for its Omaha headquarters:
- Previous landlord (Kiewit) was moving
- Charlie suggested signing for 20 years "considering"
- Berkshire now occupying two floors instead of one

> "I just want you to know that your management is loosening up just a little bit."

### Focus on Operating Earnings

Buffett warned against focusing on GAAP bottom line due to mark-to-market rules:
> "What I worry about is that not everybody studied accounting in school... I regard these bottom line figures as potentially being harmful to our shareholders."

### Kraft Heinz

Kraft Heinz had not filed its 10K, creating unusual reporting situation.
