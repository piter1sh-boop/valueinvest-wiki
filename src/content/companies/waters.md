# Waters Corporation

## Overview

Waters Corporation is a scientific instruments company specializing in chromatography (separation of chemical compounds) and mass spectrometry. The company serves pharmaceutical, chemical, and healthcare industries with precision analytical instruments.

## Why Fundsmith Admires It

### Dr. Udit Batra's Leadership

Julian Robbins at the 2026 AGM named Dr. Udit Batra (CEO of Waters) as the CEO he most admires:

> "Dr. Batra came in to Waters in September 2020. This was a company we had owned under the previous-but-one chief executive, when it had been quite successful, and then throughout the tenure of the immediately previous chief executive, when it had been much less successful.

> He was very numbers-based. He didn't put out any snappy-named plans. He just went to people and said, 'Why don't we try something a little bit different?' The results were extraordinary in an incredibly quick period of time."

### What Makes Him Exceptional

- **Numbers-based approach** — no grand strategy presentations
- **Practical execution** — "finding out where the equipment is, how long they've had it, and 'don't come back and be very proud that they've had it for 25 years — sell them a new one'"
- **Quick results** — extraordinary outcomes in short timeframe

### Recent Acquisition

Waters just acquired a flow cytometry business from Becton Dickinson. Robbins noted: "We're buying it from Becton Dickinson, a company we once owned and sold, and there's never been a day I regretted selling it."

This gives Fundsmith confidence in the acquisition.
