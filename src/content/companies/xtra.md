# XTRA Lease

**Founded:** 1980s
**Acquired by Berkshire:** 2001
**Status:** Berkshire Hathaway subsidiary

## Overview

XTRA Lease is a major provider of trucking trailers and storage containers for lease in the United States. The company operates a large fleet of trailers and containers serving the transportation and storage industries.

## Business

### Equipment Leasing

- Semi-trailers for trucking companies
- Storage containers for shipping and storage
- Specialty equipment for specific industries

### Fleet Size

XTRA maintains one of the largest trailer fleets in the United States:
- Over 100,000 trailers
- Strategic locations nationwide
- Comprehensive maintenance services

## Why It Fits Berkshire

### Capital-Light Business Model

Trailer leasing doesn't require significant capital compared to owning assets:
- Customers pay for usage
- Equipment returns after lease term
- Smaller capital requirements than trucking

### Counter-Cyclical Potential

Trailer demand can increase during economic expansions when trucking demand rises.
