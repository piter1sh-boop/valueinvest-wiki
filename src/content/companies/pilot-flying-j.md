# Pilot Flying J

**Pilot Flying J** is the largest truck stop chain in North America, operating over 750 locations across the United States and Canada. Berkshire Hathaway acquired a 38.6% stake in Pilot Flying J in 2014 through a partnership with the Haslam family, who retained majority ownership.

## Overview

Pilot Flying J provides services to the trucking industry:

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Fuel** | Diesel and gasoline for trucks and cars |
| **Truck Service** | Repairs, maintenance, tire services |
| **Travel Centers** | Restaurants, showers, lounges |
| **Fleet Services** | Fuel card programs, fleet management |

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Stake Acquired** | 38.6% (2014) |
| **Haslam Family** | ~50%+ stake, managing |
| **Locations** | 750+ across US/Canada |
| **Annual Revenue** | ~$20B+ |
| **CEO** | Jimmy Haslam (Haslam family) |

## The Partnership

### Haslam Family

Pilot Flying J was founded by "Big Jim" Haslam and is now run by his son Jimmy Haslam:

> "Jimmy Haslam is working hard every day to grow this business and treat customers right."

### Berkshire's Role

Berkshire's 38.6% stake represents:
- A long-term investment in the truck stop industry
- Partnership with the Haslam family
- Attractive returns from fuel and services

## Why Truck Stops Have Moats

### Essential Service

Truckers need:
- Fuel (diesel is essential for freight)
- Rest stops for hours-of-service compliance
- Maintenance and repairs on the road

### Location, Location, Location

Truck stop economics depend on:
- Highway visibility and access
- Competitive fuel storage
- Scale of operations

### Fleet Relationships

Pilot Flying J serves:
- Major trucking fleets (Swift, Werner, etc.)
- Owner-operators
- Automotive travelers

## The Haslam Family Bet

### Jimmy Haslam's Wager

In 2013, Buffett made a notable bet with Jimmy Haslam:
- Buffett bet Haslam $2 billion that Haslam couldn't grow Pilot Flying J significantly
- Haslam accepted and put personal money on the line
- Buffett later said this was a "moron bet" if he lost

> "Jimmy Haslam put up a billion dollars that he could make the business a lot bigger. If he wins, I'll have made a mistake."

## Business Performance

### Scale Advantages

Pilot Flying J's dominance provides:
- Volume discounts from fuel suppliers
- Fleet card network effects
- Brand recognition for travelers

### Growth Initiatives

The company has expanded:
- New location development
- Fleet services expansion
- Diesel exhaust fluid (DEF) stations

## Legal Issues

### 2013 Investigation

Pilot Flying J faced a federal investigation in 2013 regarding diesel fuel rebate fraud:
- Company cooperated fully
- Several employees were involved
- Haslam family took action against those responsible

Buffett supported the Haslams throughout:
> "Jimmy Haslam is a good man dealing with a bad situation."
