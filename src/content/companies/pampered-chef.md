# Pampered Chef

**Founded:** 1980 (by Doris Christopher)
**Acquired by Berkshire:** 2002
**CEO:** Doris Christopher (founder)
**Status:** Berkshire Hathaway subsidiary

## Overview

Pampered Chef is a direct-sales company specializing in kitchenware, food products, and cookbooks. The company is famous for its in-home cooking demonstrations conducted by a network of independent sales consultants.

## The Business Model

### Direct Sales

Pampered Chef operates through:
- Independent consultants who host in-home demonstrations
- Cooking shows and kitchen parties
- Online sales platform

### Product Line

- Cookware (pots, pans, cutting boards)
- Kitchen tools and gadgets
- Food storage products
- Cookbooks and recipe content

## Why Berkshire Bought It

### Doris Christopher

Buffett praised the founder repeatedly:
> "Doris Christopher built Pampered Chef. It's a wonderful business."

### Business Characteristics

- Strong brand in kitchenware
- Consultant network creates personal relationships
- Repeat customers through product quality
