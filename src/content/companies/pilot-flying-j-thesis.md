# Pilot Flying J - Investment Thesis

### Essential Service

Truckers need:
- Fuel (diesel is essential for freight)
- Rest stops for hours-of-service compliance
- Maintenance and repairs on the road

### Location, Location, Location

Truck stop economics depend on:
- Highway visibility and access
- Competitive fuel storage
- Scale of operations

### Fleet Relationships

Pilot Flying J serves:
- Major trucking fleets (Swift, Werner, etc.)
- Owner-operators
- Automotive travelers
