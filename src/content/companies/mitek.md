# MiTek

**Founded:** 1955
**Acquired by Berkshire:** 2001 (through acquisition of a Berkshire partnership)
**CEO:** Pete McCausland
**Status:** Berkshire Hathaway subsidiary

## Overview

MiTek is a global manufacturer of metal connectors, engineered products, and software for the construction industry. The company serves residential and commercial builders, providing critical structural components and engineering solutions.

## Business

### Products

- Metal connectors and brackets
- Engineering software
- Structural steel components
- Truss and panel machinery

### Markets

- Residential construction
- Commercial construction
- Industrial builders
