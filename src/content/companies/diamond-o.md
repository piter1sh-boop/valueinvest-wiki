# Diamond-O

**Acquired by Berkshire:** 1990s
**Status:** Berkshire Hathaway subsidiary (formerly)

## Overview

Diamond-O was a Canadian fishing company that Berkshire Hathaway acquired. The company operated in the fishing industry, primarily in the Atlantic salmon and other fish markets.

## Why Berkshire Acquired It

### Unique Business

Fishing operations have unique characteristics:
- Renewable resource
- Limited entry/exit
- Commodity pricing
- Operating leverage

### Small Acquisition

Diamond-O represented a small, opportunistic acquisition rather than a major transformative deal.

## Performance

The fishing industry is notoriously difficult:
- Commodity price volatility
- Environmental factors
- Regulatory challenges
