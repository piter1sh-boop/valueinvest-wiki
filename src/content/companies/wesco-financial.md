# Wesco Financial Corporation

**Wesco Financial Corporation** was an 80%-owned subsidiary of Blue Chip Stamps, operating as a savings and loan association. It was managed by Louis Vincenti and operated with the same conservative philosophy that characterized all of Buffett's insurance operations.

## History

### Blue Chip Stamps Affiliation

Wesco became affiliated with Blue Chip Stamps:
- Blue Chip Stamps held 80% of Wesco
- Blue Chip was itself a major Berkshire affiliate
- Operated under the "cigar butt" investing philosophy

### Conservative Operations

Wesco operated a traditional savings and loan:
- Accepted deposits from customers
- Made mortgage loans
- Maintained conservative lending standards

## Leadership

### Louis Vincenti

Louis Vincenti was president of Wesco Financial:
- Ran the company with conservative principles
- Focused on maintaining capital adequacy
- Operated independently but aligned with Buffett's philosophy
