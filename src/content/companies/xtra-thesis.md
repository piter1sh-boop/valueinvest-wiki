# XTRA Lease - Investment Thesis

### Capital-Light Business Model

Trailer leasing doesn't require significant capital compared to owning assets:
- Customers pay for usage
- Equipment returns after lease term
- Smaller capital requirements than trucking

### Counter-Cyclical Potential

Trailer demand can increase during economic expansions when trucking demand rises.
